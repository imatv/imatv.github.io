Rissman Memory Lab

    I currently work as a research assistant in a cognitive neuroscience lab at UCLA.
My current research project that I am helping with deals with Under the supervision of Dr. Jesse Rissman, I am working with Nicco Reggente, a Ph.D. graduate student, on using functional magnetic resonance imaging (fMRI) and diffusion tensor imaging (DTI) data from the Human Connectome Project (HCP) to account for individual differences in fluid intelligence, memory strength, and reward. Currently, I write Matlab and shell scripts to perform complex analyses over many subjects and examining the correlations to their behavioral scores on various IQ tests and tasks. For more details about the research that goes on in the lab, visit the lab page!
