B.S. Computer Science; Minor in Cognitive Science

VR Developer, Researcher, Hacker, Archer

Currently a 4th year at UCLA, I am interested in furthering the evolution of virtual and augmented realities (VR/AR). Ever since I received my pre-order of the Oculus Rift DK1 the summer after my high school graduation, I knew that VR/AR would become my passion and interest for years to come.

I currently work in two psychology labs as a programmer and research assistant, investigating neurological markers of human intelligence, testing effects of learning in a virtual environment, and using VR to study the visual illusions that occur with skewed/tilted rooms.

I also go to hackathons, where my teams and I have built really cool projects, like a mind-controlled car, full-body VR, AR search, heart rate-based FPS, and more!

In my free time, I shoot arrows and play video games.
